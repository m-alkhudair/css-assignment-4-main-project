About the Centers

Animla Center is a non-profit animal rescue and rehabilitation organization, with four centers located in different location around the country.

Every center is specialized in it's own particular area, and offers many opportunities for volunteering and training opportunities.

Red Sea marine life rescue center

About the center

The Red Sea marine life center is located in the costal city of Jeddah, overlooking the Red Sea. It's primary concern is the rescue, rehabilitation, and release of marine animals. This includes sharks, marine mammals, and marine reptiles.

Main activities

The activities include taking in calls from the coast guard as well as concerned citizens regarding the sighting of marine animals in distress. The center works closely with marine research centers.

Endangerd animals rescue and rehabilitation center

About the center

The endangerd animals center's main objective is to provide support to the wild life centers around the country and education about the list of endangerd animals in the country. The center is located in Riyadh.

Main activities

The main activities include education and awareness about the list of endangerd animals and their roles in the ecosystem. Activities also include visiting nature reserves and learning more about the natural habitat.

Emergency veterinary medical center

About the center

The emergency veterinary center is equipt with cutting edge medical technology, and has the necessary resources to handle the toughest animal emergencies. Center has an international team of medical professionals ready for action.

Main activities

The activities include treating wild and domestic animals, and offering medical support to the other Animal centers.

Lost pet rescue center

About the center

The lost pet rescue center is concerned with domestic and stray animals, and has a large facility for housing and taking care of these animals. It's much more relax and less high-stakes than the other centers, so it's suitable for beginner volunteers.

Main activities

Taking calls for lost pets, employing systemic search methods, and rescueing distressed stray animals.
